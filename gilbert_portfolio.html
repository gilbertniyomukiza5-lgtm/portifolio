Who is Gilbert?

A driven young developer from Rwanda combining technical education with real-world ambition.

Gilbert Niyomukiza is a dedicated Software Development student at APEKI Tumba TVET, located in Rwanda. He is currently completing an internship that runs from April 20 to May 5, 2026, gaining hands-on experience in the tech industry.
